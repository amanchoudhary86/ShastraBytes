Community Guidelines

Welcome to ShastraBytes Community

To ensure a positive and productive learning environment for everyone, please follow these guidelines:

1. Respect and Courtesy

  • Treat all community members with respect and kindness
  • Use appropriate language and maintain a professional tone
  • Be patient with learners at different skill levels

2. Learning and Collaboration

  • Share knowledge and help others learn
  • Ask thoughtful questions and provide constructive answers
  • Collaborate on projects and share resources

3. Academic Integrity

  • Complete tests and assessments honestly
  • Don't share test answers or cheat
  • Give proper credit when using others' work

4. Content Guidelines

  • Share relevant and educational content
  • Avoid spam, self-promotion, or off-topic discussions
  • Respect intellectual property rights

5. Privacy and Safety

  • Protect your personal information
  • Report inappropriate behavior or content
  • Don't share others' personal information
Note: Violations of these guidelines may result in warnings, temporary restrictions, or account suspension.
